Recipe Roulette

Welcome to Recipe Roulette

The app that let's you try your luck with whatever ingredient(s) you may have lying around.

It's as simple as...

1

Enter the ingredient(s) into the search field and click the search button.

2

A list of 6 randomly chosen recipes containing your ingredient(s) will pop up.

3

Click a recipe bullet to get a more detailed description and a list of ingredients.

4

If your in the mood to live dangerously, click the 'Try Your Luck' button to have a recipe randomly selected for you.
